LOYAL CUSTOMER PROGRAM

To show our appreciation to customers who trust and choose Matie Cake, we offer exclusive rewards for our loyal customers.

This program applies at our physical stores and our online store.

A. HOW TO REGISTER

• In-store purchases: Our staff will assist with member registration when you complete your first purchase.

• Online orders: When you place your first order on Matie Cake's website, the system will automatically create a member account based on the phone number used for the order.
