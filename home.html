About Us

Matie Cake was born from the passion and desire to create high-quality handmade cakes, preserving traditional flavors while bringing a contemporary touch. Starting with small batches from a home kitchen, the brand gradually shaped its identity: meticulous ingredient selection, sophisticated flavor balance, and creative presentation.

Through continuous research, testing, and refinement, Matie Cake entered the market with the mission to create cakes that are not only delicious but also carry emotional value. The motto “Giving delicate gifts, sending love” has guided every aspect of our work: from production to packaging design and customer experience.

From the early challenging days until today, Matie Cake has grown steadily, building a trusted reputation in the handmade cake community. Our products are now recognized as a symbol of sophistication, making them the perfect choice for gifting on meaningful occasions.
