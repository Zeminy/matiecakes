Description

Flancheese Donut is an upgraded version of the familiar flan, where the smoothness of the flan layer meets the slightly salty and fatty taste of cheese. With one bite, you will immediately feel the sweet melting blend with the rich cheese aftertaste, both new and addictive. The cake is handmade every day, no preservatives, guaranteed fresh, safe, and delicious. This is the perfect choice for those who love unique desserts, wanting to find a balance between sweetness and richness. Enjoying Flancheese Donut with a cup of tea or coffee will bring a complete experience, gentle but sophisticated enough to "close the deal" on any date.
